Death Has a Test Tomorrow Morning

The world sees Sawa as just another run-of-the-mill schoolgirl, but the underworld fears her explosive talents as an instrument of death. Trapped in a vicious cycle of violence and abuse, the young assassin lives every day like it's her last. But everything changes when a chance encounter shows her that there might be a life beyond the suffering. Unfortunately, the bloodred chains that bind Sawa don't plan to loosen up their grip anytime soon.

Director Yasuomi Umetsu (Mezzo Forte, Robot Carnival) and Studio ARMS (Elfen Lied, MAOYU) unleash a visceral torrent of sex and hyperviolence in this infamous cult classic! This definitive edition of the high-octane thriller includes the Original US, International, and Uncut versions of the film–all presented in high definition!

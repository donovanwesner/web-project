About Discotek Media

What do we do?

Discotek Media is a boutique home video company based in central Florida. We release the finest Japanese anime, live-action film, tokusatsu classics, and cult animation on Blu-ray, 4K UHD, and streaming.

Where can I find your titles?

Titles released from Discotek Media can be found at the following fine retailers:
  • Crunchyroll
  • Amazon
  • Diabolik DVD
  • Robert's Anime Corner Store
Please note that no retailers will have all of our titles all the time. If you would like to see Discotek Media titles available from more retailers, contact them and let them know.
